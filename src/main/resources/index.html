Format of the day

All riders are required to register once they arrive at the start point. Once you are registered you will be asked to wait for the briefing. Once the briefing has ended riders are free to start the ride. Please note. this is an independent ride. Group will set off together, feel free to ride at a pace you feel comfortable, whether it’s in a group or solo. Remember the challenge is to complete all 3 stages within 24 hours

The event is split into 3 stages:

  • Stage 1: London to Cambridge
  • Stage 2: Cambridge to Oxford
  • Stage 3: Oxford to London

The ride will start and finish in Altab Ali Park. More details on the route will be available closer to the event.

Meeting Point: Altab Ali Park Adler St, London E1 1FD

  • Registration: 5am
  • Start Time: 5:30am

What will be provided on the day?

Support: H&K CC will be providing partial support on the day, which means riders will only be picked up in any case your ride needs to be abandoned for any unforeseen circumstances, riders will be taken to the end point of that stage.

Snacks: Cambridge

Snacks will be provided at the end of stage 1.

Lunch: Oxford

Lunch will be provided at the end of stage 2.

Specific Dietary Requirements: If you have specific dietary requirements, each rider would be obliged to let the organisers know prior to the event to ensure we can meet your needs

Essential Kit

  1. Helmet
  2. Fully charged lights
  3. Two inner tubes, pump and tyre leavers.
  4. Rucksacks can be brought; however please note you will only have access to the bag at each end stage. The support vehicle will only be available at the finish line up to a specific time. If you finish your ride where the support vehicle has left the end point, you will be able to retrieve your bag the following day.
  5. Your well-deserved Nutters Ride medal will be given out by the support team at the finishing line up to a specific time (exact time: TBC). Any riders finishing after that time can collect theirs next day or we can arrange posting it.

WhatsApp Group

Temporary WhatsApp group will be set up 2 weeks prior to the event. This will only be used to post any relevant updates
